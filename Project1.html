Engine Servo Valves

Designed and fabricated multiple servo-actuated valves with custom 3D-printed housings to regulate engine propellant flow.

Embedded • Mechanical • Actuation • Testing

Overview

The objective was to design and fabricate servo-actuated fixtures for specific ball valves used in main engine operations, including regulating propellant flow during hot-fire testing, while characterizing and tabulating actuation performance to verify that response times met operational requirements.
