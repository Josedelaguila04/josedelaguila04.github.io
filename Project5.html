L1 Rocket

Designed, fabricated, and successfully launched an L1-certified rocket, including hands-on manufacturing of the full airframe and the design and 3D printing of the nose cone.

Fabrication • Simulation • Design • Testing

Overview

The objective of this personal project was to design, fabricate, and successfully launch an L1-class rocket to obtain Level 1 certification. I collaborated with a teammate to simulate the vehicle in OpenRocket, evaluate stability margins, and predict flight performance using an H100 solid motor. To improve structural performance and gain hands-on experience with composites, we fabricated the fins using composite fiber layups and epoxy. The project also included recovery system validation through drop testing.
